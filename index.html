About

Hi my name is Emily I’m 18 years old and I’m from the Bronx New York. I’m Mexican and colombian. I used to model in runway shows but I felt like it wasn’t my passion. So I moved on to filmmaking and photography. I also created a clothing brand when I was 15 called “Wavy fits” that was when I knew fashion was my passion. Growing up my parents always told me I had to be a doctor or a nurse but I never saw myself doing that because it was just too much work and school. So wavy fits took over till now. I would create fashion pieces with my merch and just see what I could do with it. At the end of the day I wasn’t completely happy with my clothing brand. The name just didn’t sit well with me now that I’m 18. It felt childish. So now I’m working on a brand that I could rep and eat and breathe it without feeling insecure about it because it’s my brand and something I should be proud of. I do believe that life is filled with trials and errors and the first time won’t be great but I’m sure the third or fourth time it could be perfected. Time and patience is everything. Im now 21 years old and my brand is called Perspectives I have now sold out more than 5 drops. Everyone I know has perspective merchandise. Im so happy I took the time to build my brand to get to where I am now patience is everything and my graphic design works have become so much better and have been helping my brand tremendously. I hope to see where my life takes me next.

Emily Calderon
